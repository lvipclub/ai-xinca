About ai.xinca.com

HVAC Controls Engineering Knowledge Hub

ai.xinca.com is the knowledge hub for HVAC controls engineering professionals. We cover dampers, actuators, valves, sensors, and building automation — not chillers, boilers, or ducting. If you're specifying damper actuators, sizing control valves, selecting VAV controllers, or integrating BACnet networks, this is your resource.

What We Offer

  • ▸AI Chatbot — Ask HVAC101 questions, get answers backed by engineering knowledge with citations.
  • ▸FAQ Knowledge Base — An organized library of HVAC controls knowledge, structured from real-world conversations into searchable articles covering air-side, water-side, controls, and product domains.
  • ▸Cross-Brand Tools — Pairing matrices and compatibility tools — launching soon.
  • ▸Knowledge Articles — Engineering articles and practical guides for HVAC controls professionals. Browse our growing library of in-depth content.

About XINCA

XINCA is an ecommerce partner of authorized HVAC controls suppliers. We help engineers and contractors source dampers, actuators, valves, sensors, and building automation products from leading manufacturers including Belimo, Honeywell, Johnson Controls, and Siemens.
